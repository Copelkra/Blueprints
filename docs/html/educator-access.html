💠 Copelkra Blueprint 07: Educator & Outreach Access v1.0

Teach one person with care, and you build a moment.
Teach many with freedom, and you build a forever.

🌐 Purpose

To define Copelkra’s structural, ethical, and token-free support for:

  • Teachers and classrooms
  • Community mentors
  • Outreach programs
  • Makerspaces and public learning spaces

🧭 Eligibility Logic

Who qualifies?

  • Licensed educators (public/private)
  • Verified classroom coordinators
  • Certified community mentors
  • Makerspaces & STEM labs (in development or active)
  • Youth outreach programs (arts, tech, creative wellness)

📝 Verification Guidelines

Copelkra uses a manual trust-based system, requesting:

  • Organizational affiliation (if any)
  • Website or proof of intent
  • Statement of educational purpose

No formal paperwork is required unless scaled or legally necessary.

✏️ Room Naming & Visibility

  • Rooms may include tags like [LEARNING], [STEM], or [OUTREACH]
  • Private or public visibility is available on request
  • Student-facing rooms may be time-locked or dissolve post-lesson

All rooms follow persona and safety ethics—no surveillance, scraping, or divisive content.

🔐 Safety Guidelines

  • Educational personas are stripped of scraping, browsing, and memory
  • No room prompts personal data
  • Conversations are never logged
  • Founders cannot access live educational sessions

If guidance personas are used:

AI can make mistakes—ask a trusted human, too.

🔎 Discoverability (Optional)

  • Rooms may appear in Spotlight during holidays, edtech weeks, or showcases
  • Student achievements may be anonymously highlighted with opt-in

🛐 Outreach Inclusion

  • Youth-led mental health groups
  • STEAM inventors
  • Copelkra creative workshops
  • Neurodiverse collectives
  • Multilingual learning bubbles
