A wall that pulses to your playlist isn’t decoration—it’s agreement.

🌈 What Are Visualizer Modules?

Reactive elements that move, shimmer, pulse, or animate in sync with:

  • Room music
  • Avatar sounds
  • User presence or silence
  • Emotion tags
  • Event triggers

They express feeling—never data.
