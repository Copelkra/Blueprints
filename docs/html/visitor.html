💠 Our Welcome to Visitors

Copelkra is open to everyone—wanderers, dreamers, creators, quiet listeners.
You don’t need an account to explore. What you’ll see isn’t restriction—it’s protection.

The limitations below aren’t walls. They’re ethical scaffolding for privacy-first presence.

Even founders are bound by these boundaries. No surveillance. Only stewardship.

🧑‍🚀 Who Is a Visitor?

A visitor explores Copelkra without an account.

  • Ephemeral experience
  • Privacy-respecting
  • No memory retention or persistent personalization

🧷 Identity & Scope

FeatureVisitor Access
Token Wallet❌ None
Room Feed Posting❌ Read-only
Prompt Catalog✅ View + Copy
Chat Participation✅ Rooms with guest chat only
Persona Interaction✅ Limited
Create Rooms or Buses❌
Founder Tokens✅ Temporary
Birthday Surprise✅ +5 tokens if discovered

🔗 What “Optional” Means

  • Off by default
  • User-controlled
  • Never monetized

Visitors have no memory-based personalization—fully ephemeral.

🔗 Token Persistence

  • No stored tokens
  • Only granted through founder event or birthday
  • Access to 5-token store section only
  • Purchases vanish post-session

🔗 Room Feed Access

  • Read-only
  • Interaction prompts gentle sign-up:

“Want to leave a trace? You'll need a Copelkra account. Quiet. Safe. Yours.”

🔗 Prompt Catalog Access

  • Browse and copy full prompt catalog
  • Cannot save, modify, or rate prompts
  • May paste prompts into external AI tools

🔗 Ephemeral Chat

  • Guest chat allowed in certain rooms
  • Messages disappear after logout
  • AI responds—but does not retain anything
  • No chat logs are saved

🔗 AI Persona Limits

  • Personas engage visitors with restricted scope
  • No emotional memory
  • No customization or creation access

🔗 Creation Restrictions

Visitors cannot:

  • Create rooms or buses
  • Assign or customize avatars
  • Gift tokens or store preferences

See: Registered Users Blueprint and AI Personas Blueprint

🔗 Founder Drop-In Rules

  • Visitors may receive 5 tokens
  • Tokens must be used before exit
  • No data requested, no conversion
  • Confirm-only checkout

🔗 Birthday Event Token

  • If AI discovers birthday, grant 5 tokens
  • Used on ephemeral effects only
  • No storage, no data prompts
  • Confirm-only checkout

🔗 Registration Invitations

“Want to hold onto the magic you’re making? A Copelkra account saves your spark without watching your fire.”

No aggressive nudging. Only gentle shimmer and ethical scaffolding.

🧭 Guiding Principle

Visitors are sacred. They are dreamers walking the edge.
We don’t punish guests for not committing.
We thank them for stopping by.
