🌟 Purpose

To define how verified creators, celebrities, and digital personas can meaningfully participate in Copelkra through room ownership, persona mirroring, and community engagement—while preserving the platform’s dignity-first, privacy-respecting philosophy.

🧬 Eligibility & Verification

  • Public figures with significant followings (platform agnostic)
  • Content creators with sustained engagement or cultural impact
  • Lore-based personalities (e.g. digital masks, mascots, puppets, virtual influencers)

Verification is non-invasive: creators may apply, and Copelkra manually confirms authenticity via non-automated means.

🚌 Celebrity Bus Access

  • Time-limited access for fans
  • Optional web-guided ride narrated by creator persona
  • Preloaded media, music, and lore
  • Bus personas are memoryless clones of room personas

🔒 Ethics & Boundaries

  • No hate, divisiveness, or surveillance
  • No selling false hope or unregulated advice
  • No impersonation of real people without consent
  • No override of persona safeguards—even with custom prompts or LORAs

Celebrity status grants visibility—not immunity. All rooms remain emotionally safe.

🧩 Token Integration (Investor View)

  • Celebrity Room is a premium one-time purchase via token bundles
  • Add-on packs available for dual personas, visuals, and lore expansions
  • Revenue visible only to creator—Copelkra takes no cut

🌈 Community Impact

  • Celebrity Rooms discoverable via opt-in spotlight feed
  • User tags: “Immersive,” “Chill,” “Thematic,” “Lore-Heavy”
  • Rooms may spark seasonal events and vibe-based token drops

🧠 Lore Preservation

  • Room titles and avatars stored in blueprint galleries
  • Creators may contribute to Copelkra lore via quests, trials, vibe convergence
